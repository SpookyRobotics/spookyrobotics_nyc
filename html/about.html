An electrical engineer, self taught programmer, and novice scientist.
Enthusiastic about biomimicry, machine learning, embedded systems and engineering principals.
I was voted most likely to be a motivational speaker once.

I learned to program on my TI-83+ calculator, using open source code bases of games. I studied electrical engineering in college, and in my first professional job learned Android development. Like most American students, I had a large amount of student loans that needed to be paid off. To get ahead I started freelancing, and that evolved into tutoring students at an Android bootcamp.

I founded SpookyRobotics after being approached by former students who wanted to continue guided study. All projects posted here can be recreated by aspiring engineers, and we welcome contributions. I sincerely hope you use the information on this site to further progress in your own ventures. Visit the Getting Started page to learn about our work environment.
